Remarks

Envelopes are applied on top of the channel's attributes, as set via ChannelSetAttribute(Int32, ChannelAttribute, Single). In the case of Frequency and Volume, the final sample rate and volume is a product of the channel attribute and the envelope. While in the Pan case, the final panning is a sum of the channel attribute and envelope.
