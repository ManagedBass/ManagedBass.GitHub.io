ManagedBass.Cd Namespace

Classes

BassCd

BassCd is a BASS addon enabling digital streaming and ripping of audio CDs. Also includes analog playback routines.

Structs

CDInfo

Used with GetInfo(Int32, out CDInfo) to retrieve information on a drive.
TOC

Used with GetTOC(Int32, TOCMode, out TOC) to retrieve the TOC from a CD.
TOCTrack

Represents one track of a CD's TOC (see TOC).
